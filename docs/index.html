Explore Laos' AMR & AMU Dashboards

Welcome to LaoAMR.net, your central platform for essential data on antimicrobial resistance (AMR) and antimicrobial use (AMU) in Laos.

AMR Dashboard – Track & Analyze resistance trends

The AMR Dashboard is designed to help clinicians, microbiologists, and policymakers monitor antimicrobial resistance patterns across key pathogens. This tool provides healthcare workers and policymakers with user-friendly and flexible summary tables and plots. By offering the most recent and standardized AMR data, this dashboard supports evidence-based decision-making to strengthen antimicrobial resistance surveillance and response efforts in Laos.

AMU Dashboard – Understand Antimicrobial Use

The AMU Dashboard provides clinicians, pharmacists, nurses and policymakers with easy access to the hospital's data, enabling them to track antimicrobial use and prescribing trends efficiently. This dashboard also automatically generates reports for the Ministry of Health, streamlining reporting and improving communication between healthcare facilities and authorities. By supporting antimicrobial stewardship programs, it ensures that antimicrobials are used responsibly, helping reduce the risk of resistance.

These dashboards provide data-driven insights to support antimicrobial stewardship to combat AMR.
